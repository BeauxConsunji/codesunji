StudySmart

Click here to visit the website

Problem

Highschool can be incredibly challenging and students often feel that there’s too much content to revise and so little time. To make it worse, some students may be unaware that they’re actually using ineffective study techniques which cause them to waste their time revising when they could be spending that time on themselves or getting through way more content.

Description

StudySmart is an initiative by students for students to bring you evidence-based study tools and techniques to achieve the grade you deserve. For example, the retrospective timetable feature allows students to dynamically revise for their exams by applying the spaced repetition technique, which takes advantage of that psychological spacing effect which is that more content can be encoded into long-term memory when learning is spaced out.
